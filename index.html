Industries we serve

Domestic PNG
The household use of gas has picked up in India, especially when multiple use of natural gas in the houses was demonstrated. The gas can be used for cooking, water heating, space heating, air conditioning, refrigeration and power generation. The concept of a single switch solution in the household sector is picking up momentum. This concept provides that a household could be a self contained gas user to meet its all energy needs at one go.

Commercial PNG
Industries with the requirement less than 50,000 SCM per day can be catered for supplying fuel under City Gas Distribution.Cost and supply of energy can play a significant role in industrial operations. A broad range of industries like chemicals, Food processing, Engineering Goods, power generation, Glass industries etc are using Industrial PNG. Gas is used by these industries for application like Heating-Boiler, Thermic Fluid Heater, Furnace; Cooling- Absorption Chiller; Power- Gas Engine and Gas Turbine; Process- for dryers,oven etc

Industrial PNG
Requirement of Fuel for Commercial Units like hotels, restaurants, malls shopping complex etc can be categorized for commercial PNG. In the commercial sector, city gas has been found to be very useful in applications like cooking, air conditioning and power generation. In fact, a concept of Combined Heat and Power (CHP) is becoming popular in India. This system has much higher thermal efficiency as compared to other conventional services.
